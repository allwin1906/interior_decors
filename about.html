Our Story

Bringing Your Vision to Life

Sr. Muthukumar Interior Decorators is a premier interior design firm specializing in creating elegant, highly functional, and aesthetically pleasing spaces. With over a decade of collective expertise in the industry, we have established ourselves as trusted interior contractors in Trichy, Karur, Erode, and surrounding regions in Tamil Nadu.

We believe that every home has a story to tell. Our dedicated team works closely with each client to ensure their unique personality and requirements are meticulously reflected in our designs. From custom modular kitchens and sophisticated wardrobe designs to captivating false ceilings and reliable aluminium partitions, our craftsmanship stands the test of time.
