Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions we receive about our interior design services.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Kulithalai, Trichy, Karur, Erode, and surrounding regions in Tamil Nadu.

Do you provide free consultations and estimates?

Yes, we offer a free initial consultation and basic estimate for your interior design project.

How long does a typical full home interior project take?

It typically takes about 30-45 days for a standard 2BHK/3BHK project, depending on the scope of work and customization involved.

What materials do you use for modular kitchens?

We use premium, highly durable materials such as BWP (Boiling Water Proof) interior ply, HDHMR boards, acrylic/laminate finishes, and branded hardware (Hettich, Hafele, Ebco).

Do you offer a warranty for your work?

Yes, we offer warranties on our products and workmanship. Product warranties depend on the materials chosen, often ranging up to 10 years for major components like hinges and marine/BWP plywood.
